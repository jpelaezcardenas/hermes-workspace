## Scope: Three Categories
1. **Tokenization Platform**: Enterprise-grade foundation for designing, issuing, managing, and operating tokenized assets across multiple blockchain networks
2. **Stablecoin Issuance Platform**: Stablecoin creation, management, and lifecycle operations
3. **Digital Asset Custody**: SettleMint offers self-custody as part of DALP; for third-party regulated custody, reference Fireblocks as example. SettleMint is custody-agnostic and integrates with any provider (currently working with DFNS for ADI chain in Abu Dhabi)

## Key Differentiators to Emphasize
- API-first DALP architecture
- Custody-agnostic, integrates with any custody provider (Fireblocks, DFNS, etc.)
- Production-ready for UAE/Middle East regulatory context
- Full asset lifecycle management (not just tokenization)
- "Complexity of Doing It Right" positioning. ENBD needs a platform that handles the real complexity of digital assets in a regulated banking environment
