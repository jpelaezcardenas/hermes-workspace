# 1. Executive Summary

## 1.1 Commercial Overview

SettleMint is pleased to present this commercial proposal for the Digital Asset Lifecycle Platform (DALP) as the foundational infrastructure for JSE's digital asset trading and regulated market initiative. This proposal outlines the investment required to deliver a production-grade platform supporting issuance, compliance enforcement, custody orchestration, atomic settlement, and automated servicing for tokenized financial instruments.

The proposed commercial model combines a subscription-based platform license with implementation services and ongoing support. This model aligns SettleMint's incentives with JSE's success by providing continuous platform improvement while maintaining predictable cost planning.

## 1.2 Investment Summary

The total investment for the initial three-year period comprises:

- Platform subscription (Year 1)
- Implementation services
- Support and maintenance (Year 1)
- Estimated total Year 1 investment

Year 2 and Year 3 follow the subscription model with standard annual fees.

## 1.3 Key Commercial Principles

**Predictability:** Annual subscription fees provide predictable budgeting without unexpected costs.

**Scalability:** The platform supports controlled expansion without per-transaction or per-user licensing that creates cost friction during growth.

**Alignment:** Ongoing support and platform updates are included, ensuring the platform evolves with regulatory and market requirements.

**Risk Sharing:** Phased implementation with milestone-based payments reduces JSE's exposure while maintaining delivery momentum.
