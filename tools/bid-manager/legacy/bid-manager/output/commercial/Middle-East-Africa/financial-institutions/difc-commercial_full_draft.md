# 1. Executive Summary

## 1.1 Strategic Context

The DIFC Digital Asset Regulatory Sandbox Platform represents a strategic investment in Dubai's position as a global centre for digital asset innovation. This commercial proposal outlines the investment required to deploy and operate DALP (Digital Asset Lifecycle Platform) as the foundational infrastructure for the sandbox.

The investment case rests on three pillars. First, the platform enables DIFC to attract financial institutions and fintech companies seeking a regulated environment for digital asset innovation. Second, the sandbox creates a pathway from testing to production, reducing friction for successful participants. Third, the platform generates operational revenue through participant licensing fees.

# 2. Commercial Overview

## 2.1 Engagement Model

SettleMint proposes a platform licensing engagement with professional services for implementation. This model provides:

- **Predictable costs**: Annual licensing fees enable budget planning
- **Clear scope**: Implementation services defined in scope
- **Operational flexibility**: Rolling annual terms with appropriate notice periods
- **Shared success**: DALP licensing model aligns SettleMint's interests with DIFC's sandbox success

## 2.2 Commercial Principles

Our commercial approach is guided by three principles:

**Transparency**: All costs are clearly stated with no hidden fees. Implementation includes all activities required to achieve sandbox go-live.

**Flexibility**: Annual licensing terms provide operational flexibility. Expansion options enable scaling as the sandbox grows.

**Partnership**: We view this engagement as a partnership in building Dubai's digital asset ecosystem. Successful sandbox participants create long-term value for both parties.

# 10. Value Proposition

## 10.1 Why This Investment Makes Sense

The DALP platform represents proven, production-grade infrastructure that accelerates DIFC's digital asset sandbox ambition:

**Speed to Market**: The platform ships with all required capabilities, configured and ready. Implementation achieves sandbox go-live in 19 weeks, compared to 12-18 months for custom development.

**Production Proven**: DALP powers live deployments with tier-1 banks and sovereign entities. The platform has processed real transactions since 2016, with Saudi Arabia RER achieving national-scale deployment in January 2026.

**Regulatory Ready**: Pre-built compliance templates for MiCA, MAS, FCA, SEC, and Japan FSA reduce compliance configuration effort. DIFC-specific requirements are accommodated through the configurable compliance engine.

**Operational Efficiency**: Comprehensive monitoring, audit trails, and automation reduce operational burden. The platform requires no third-party tooling for core operations.

**Ecosystem Enablement**: The sandbox creates a platform for ecosystem development, attracting participants who contribute to DIFC's digital economy ambitions.

## 10.2 Competitive Advantages

| Advantage | Benefit |
|-----------|---------|
| EVM-Native | Operates on any EVM-compatible blockchain |
| Configuration, Not Code | Reduce custom development risk |
| Ex-Ante Compliance | Non-compliant transactions cannot exist on-chain |
| Atomic Settlement | True T+0 eliminates counterparty risk |
| Enterprise Security | ISO 27001 and SOC 2 Type II certified |
