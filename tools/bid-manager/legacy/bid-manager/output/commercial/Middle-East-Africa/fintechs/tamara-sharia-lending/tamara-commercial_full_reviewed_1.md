## Executive Summary

Tamara requires infrastructure that bridges its growing BNPL receivable portfolio with the Islamic finance investor community. Traditional bank financing limits scale and investor diversity. Building a tokenization platform internally would require 18+ months of specialized engineering and regulatory validation that Tamara cannot afford given competitive pressure.

DALP provides a ready-to-deploy platform that natively supports Sharia-compliant token structures: murabaha, wakala, and musharaka instruments are configurable through the platform's composable architecture. The compliance layer handles both SAMA/CMA regulatory requirements and Sharia supervisory board governance.
