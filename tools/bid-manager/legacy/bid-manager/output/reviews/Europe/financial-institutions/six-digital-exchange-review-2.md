**TR matrix:** Now 47 requirements (TR-001 to TR-046 plus PFMI P5 as TR-030). All marked Comply with specific evidence. Renumbering is consistent. No gaps.

**Diagrams:** 11 diagrams. Quality is good. The FINMA observer inclusion in Diagram 4 (issuance flow sequence) is a differentiating detail. Diagram 6 (XvP settlement) correctly notes FMIA Article 51 finality point.

**Writing quality:** Strong throughout. Problem-Solution-Evidence structure well-maintained. Active voice predominant. Swiss-specific content is credible and precise.

**Migration approach:** Sections 1.4 and 1.4a provide a clear, credible migration strategy. The three-phase approach (parallel operation, voluntary migration, full migration) is well-articulated and addresses SDX's primary continuity concern.
