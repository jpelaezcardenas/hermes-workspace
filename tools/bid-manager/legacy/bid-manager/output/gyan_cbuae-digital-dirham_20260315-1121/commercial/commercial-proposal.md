## Executive Summary

The CBUAE requires sovereign-grade infrastructure for the Digital Dirham programme. This commercial proposal presents SettleMint's licensing, implementation, and support model for DALP as the enabling platform, structured for the multi-phase, multi-year nature of CBDC deployment.

**Recommendation:** Sovereign tier (Tier 3) platform license with Enterprise support (24×7), phased 18-month implementation across wholesale, retail pilot, and full deployment.
