## Executive Summary

QNB requires a platform partner for production-grade tokenized sukuk issuance and servicing. This commercial proposal presents SettleMint's licensing, implementation, and support model for DALP, structured for predictable costs and transparent total cost of ownership.

**Recommendation:** Enterprise tier (Tier 2) platform license with Premium support, 19-week fixed-fee implementation, dedicated cloud deployment within Qatar-resident infrastructure.

DALP's platform licensing model charges zero per-transaction, per-token, per-distribution, or per-investor fees. For sukuk programmes where periodic distributions and compliance checks occur at high frequency, this eliminates the cost anxiety that transaction-based pricing creates.
