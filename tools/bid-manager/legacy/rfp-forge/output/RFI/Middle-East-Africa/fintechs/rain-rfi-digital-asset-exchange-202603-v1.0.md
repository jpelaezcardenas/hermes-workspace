## 1. Purpose of Inquiry

Rain is gathering market information for digital asset exchange. This RFI is exploratory and intended to test vendor maturity, implementation realism, and control-model fit.
## 2. Information Objectives

The buyer wants evidence on workflow maturity, architecture, integrations, security, resilience, regulatory fit, and commercial/delivery models. Relevant regulatory context includes CBB; market initiatives include Bahrain digital asset market development. Where Islamic-finance considerations arise, the response should explain configurable controls and approval routing without implying a forked product architecture.
## 3. Requested Vendor Input

Respondents should describe support for:
- listing and market-operations workflows
- order, trade, and settlement integration
- surveillance and compliance reporting

Responses must clearly mark current, configurable, partner-enabled, roadmap, and unsupported capabilities.
## 4. Follow-up Path

The buyer may use responses to shape a later RFP, pilot, market sounding, or internal strategy paper. Vague product marketing will be scored down.
