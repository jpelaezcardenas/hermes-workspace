## 1. Purpose of Inquiry

M-Pesa/Safaricom is gathering market information for digital asset mobile payment. This RFI is exploratory and intended to test vendor maturity, implementation realism, and control-model fit.
## 2. Information Objectives

The buyer wants evidence on workflow maturity, architecture, integrations, security, resilience, regulatory fit, and commercial/delivery models. Relevant regulatory context includes CBK, CMA Kenya, ODPC; market initiatives include mobile money innovation leadership, Pan-African remittance modernisation. Where Islamic-finance considerations arise, the response should explain configurable controls and approval routing without implying a forked product architecture.
## 3. Requested Vendor Input

Respondents should describe support for:
- mobile wallet and payment integration layers
- consumer-risk and fraud controls
- merchant acceptance and settlement reporting

Responses must clearly mark current, configurable, partner-enabled, roadmap, and unsupported capabilities.
## 4. Follow-up Path

The buyer may use responses to shape a later RFP, pilot, market sounding, or internal strategy paper. Vague product marketing will be scored down.
