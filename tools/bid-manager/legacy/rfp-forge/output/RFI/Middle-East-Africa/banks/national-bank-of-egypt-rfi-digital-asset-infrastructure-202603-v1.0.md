## 1. Purpose of Inquiry

National Bank of Egypt is gathering market information for digital asset infrastructure. This RFI is exploratory and intended to test vendor maturity, implementation realism, and control-model fit.
## 2. Information Objectives

The buyer wants evidence on workflow maturity, architecture, integrations, security, resilience, regulatory fit, and commercial/delivery models. Relevant regulatory context includes CBE, FRA; market initiatives include Egyptian financial infrastructure modernisation, regional CBDC research. Where Islamic-finance considerations arise, the response should explain configurable controls and approval routing without implying a forked product architecture.
## 3. Requested Vendor Input

Respondents should describe support for:
- core platform governance and administration
- API/event integration services
- operations, compliance, and reporting dashboards

Responses must clearly mark current, configurable, partner-enabled, roadmap, and unsupported capabilities.
## 4. Follow-up Path

The buyer may use responses to shape a later RFP, pilot, market sounding, or internal strategy paper. Vague product marketing will be scored down.
