## 1. Procurement Context

Qatar National Bank (QNB) is seeking a production-grade platform and implementation approach for tokenized sukuk. The buyer wants a defensible institutional operating model, not a demo. The programme is driven by modernise sukuk issuance workflows while preserving Sharia governance, improve investor reporting and lifecycle administration, support asset-backing evidence and entitlement clarity.
## 2. Regulatory and Market Context

The response must reflect Qatar requirements, including QCB, QFCRA, QFMA. Relevant market initiatives include regional sukuk digitisation initiatives, GCC capital market modernisation. The response must explicitly address Sharia governance, AAOIFI-aligned review, product permissibility controls, and evidence suitable for Sharia-board review.
## 3. Scope of Required Deliverables

Expected deliverables include:
- sukuk structuring templates and approval workflows
- profit distribution and redemption event automation
- Sharia review artefacts and audit-ready reporting

Vendors must distinguish shipped capability, configurable platform capability, partner-enabled scope, and roadmap items.
## 4. Response Requirements

Respondents must provide architecture, deployment model, integration approach, security controls, operational resilience, implementation plan, support model, commercial assumptions, and evidence references.
## 5. Evaluation Criteria

Evaluation will prioritize production maturity, evidence quality, operational control, implementation realism, security, governance, and clarity of scope boundaries.
