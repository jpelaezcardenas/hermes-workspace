# Regulatory Change Governance Requirement Pattern

Use this block when the buyer operates in a fast-changing regulatory environment or expects supervised rollout of digital asset services.

- **CR-REG-01:** The proposed solution shall support controlled implementation of regulatory rule changes without requiring material platform downtime. The Respondent shall describe how new reporting, investor eligibility, transfer restriction, disclosure, or workflow controls can be configured, tested, approved, and promoted to production.
- **CR-REG-02:** The Respondent shall provide evidence of governance controls for regulatory change, including version control, segregation of duties, approval workflow, audit trail retention, and rollback capability.
- **CR-REG-03:** The proposed solution should allow institution-defined compliance teams to review and approve rule changes before activation in the production environment.

Why this matters: regulated institutions need confidence that policy changes can be implemented in a controlled, auditable manner without depending on ad hoc vendor intervention or emergency production changes.
