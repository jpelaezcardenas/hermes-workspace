# Tokenization 101

## Suggested Slide Title
Tokenization Is Easy; Operating It in Production Is Hard

## Suggested Layout
- Primary: Slide 16 — summary list plus big statement
- Alternate: Slide 17 — insight plus explanation

## Key Talking Points
- Tokenization is not just minting: it includes compliance, custody, settlement, servicing, and redemption
- Most projects underestimate operational complexity until they leave pilot mode
- Regulated programs need identity, governance, auditability, and lifecycle controls from day one
- DALP is built around the full lifecycle rather than issuance alone

## Supporting Proof Points
- Core lifecycle in the narrative spans issuance, compliance, custody, settlement, and servicing
- Atomic DvP/XvP is positioned as the answer to counterparty and reconciliation risk
- Pilot-to-production path without re-architecture is a central DALP message
