## OCBC Bank

**Use case:** Security token engine for securitization, tokenization, and fractionalization of off-chain assets. Target segment: HNWIs and HENRYs. Investment products across bonds, SPVs, stocks, and real estate.

**Challenge:** Deliver innovative investment products with a secure end-user interface for tokenization, wallet management, and cash positions, and a backend with order book management and APIs to integrate with off-chain securities and cash systems.

**Solution:** SettleMint implemented a security token engine that enhanced liquidity for illiquid assets and expanded investment opportunities. The solution included a secure end-user interface and an order book management system for seamless transactions.

**Outcome:** OCBC Bank benefited from an easy-to-administer and scalable digital asset exchange platform.

## KBC Securities (Bolero Crowdfunding)

**Use case:** Equity crowdfunding platform expanded to include SME loans. Backend overhaul using smart contracts to replace manual processes.

**Challenge:** Rapid growth created significant administrative pressure; new regulations increased compliance burdens and threatened the platform's viability.

**Solution:** SettleMint overhauled the backend using smart contracts to automate issuance, lifecycle management, corporate actions, and redemption. The solution integrated digital wallets and a fiat-backed stable token to streamline on-chain transactions.

**Outcome:** Automation reduced operational costs, ensured regulatory compliance, and boosted scalability. Bolero Crowdfunding captured growth efficiently without incurring additional expenses.

---

## KBC Insurance

**Use case:** Valuing unique, high-value assets during insurance claims using NFTs.

**Challenge:** Loss assessments were slow, costly, and reactive; difficult to process claims accurately and fairly.

**Solution:** SettleMint supported KBC Insurance in creating digital "product passports" for insured assets using NFTs. Through a mobile app, customers could upload images and professional appraisal reports, which were converted into NFTs to represent the assets' value.

**Outcome:** KBC transformed its approach to asset valuation and claims processing.

## Standard Chartered Bank

**Use case:** Trading efficiency for institutional investors in high-growth regions (Asia, Africa, Middle East).

**Challenge:** Improve customer experience while reducing costs tied to intermediaries and lengthy settlement processes.

**Solution:** SettleMint collaborated on a blockchain-based Digital Virtual Exchange supporting fractional tokenization of securities (shares, bonds, currencies). Ownership changes were recorded instantly and immutably on the blockchain, eliminating the need for custody intermediaries.

**Outcome:** Faster settlement times, greater transparency, and improved liquidity.

## Reserve Bank of India – Innovation Hub

**Use case:** Multi-bank letter of credit trade finance solution.

**Challenge:** Create a fraud-proof, tamper-proof blockchain application for scalable, secure trade finance workflows; allow multiple banks to run their own nodes; ensure interoperability across networks.

**Solution:** Multi-node, multi-cloud blockchain infrastructure deployed using SettleMint's Blockchain platform. Included chain-code for user management, transaction explorer, and document uploads, along with secure UI deployment for banks and corporates.

**Outcome:** A scalable, secure, multi-party trade finance infrastructure.

## Sony Bank (Sony Group, Japan)

**Use case:** Issuance and management of stablecoins with integrated digital identity for regulated use.

**Challenge:** Build secure Web3 banking with KYC-enabled digital identity, fiat on-chain settlement, and compliance with unclear regulations within 3 months.

**Solution:** New ERC standard combining digital identity, a stablecoin engine, and asset tokenization; seamless onboarding through Privado.id while hiding technical Web3 complexity.

**Outcome:** Successful Phase 1 implementation, regulator-ready positioning, and a clear roadmap for production launch.

## Islamic Development Bank (IsDB) – Market Stabilization

**Use case:** Fully automated market stabilization system for assets used as collateral for sharia-compliant lending.

**Challenge:** Address excessive volatility without human error.

**Solution:** Advanced algorithms, predictive modelling, and smart contracts to regulate volatility.

**Outcome:** Reduced market volatility by 30–50%; greater stability and reliability for Islamic finance products; strengthened trust in sharia-compliant lending mechanisms.

## Maybank (Project Photon)

**Use case:** FX tokenization and cross-border settlement using an Exchange-versus-Payment (XvP) model.

**Solution:** MYRT token (tokenized Malaysian Ringgit) issued in a controlled environment, fully backed by fiat balances. Enables atomic cross-currency swaps with simultaneous settlement of both legs to reduce counterparty and settlement risk.

**Context:** Implemented in alignment with Maybank's digital transformation strategy and Bank Negara Malaysia's Digital Asset Innovation Hub (DAIH).

**Outcome:** Scalable foundation for production deployment and expansion into broader tokenized deposit and cross-border settlement.

## ADI – Finstreet

**Use case:** Issuance and management of tokenized equity on ADI's Abu Dhabi-based mainnet.

**Solution:** Equity token with embedded corporate action functionality (stock splits, consolidations via controlled mint and burn); on-chain voting using ERC20Votes. Upgradeable smart contracts on ADI's private network. Integration with institutional-grade custody (e.g. DFNS for transaction signing, Fireblocks integration path); structured approval workflow. Cloud-agnostic infrastructure (Terraform, Helm, managed Kubernetes in UAE/GCC).

**Outcome:** Scalable foundation for regulated equity tokenization on ADI's dedicated blockchain environment.

## Commerzbank

## Saudi Arabia RER (Real Estate Registry)

**Use case:** Country-scale blockchain infrastructure for real estate registration, fractionalization, and digital marketplace. Operated by the Real Estate Registry (RER) under the Real Estate General Authority (REGA); central to the Kingdom's digital transformation under Vision 2030.

**Context:** "Registry-as-truth" model; RER ledger as conclusive record of property rights. Full journey from listing and due diligence through identity verification, fee payment, escrow, and on-chain transfer to final update of the deed. Guided by international best-practice benchmarks adapted to Saudi institutional context.

**Solution:** SettleMint is delivery partner for the end-to-end solution: marketplace services, API gateway, blockchain and tokenization layer (powered by DALP), orchestration and integration with RER's core registry, billing, escrow, case worker, and government systems. Exposed through the unified RER API Gateway for PropTechs, banks, and developers.

**Outcome:** First country-scale blockchain infrastructure dedicated to real estate registration, fractionalization, and digital marketplace; single, regulated infrastructure for the Kingdom.
