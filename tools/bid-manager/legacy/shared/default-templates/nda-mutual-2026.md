# SettleMint Mutual NDA Template 2026

**File:** `nda-mutual-2026.docx`  
**Added:** 2026-03-25  
**Source:** Gyan  

## Purpose
Standard mutual non-disclosure agreement template for SettleMint NV. Use this whenever a prospect, partner, or vendor requires an NDA before a business discussion.

## Key Details
- **SettleMint party:** SettleMint NV (n° 0661.674.810), Philipssite 5 bus 1, 3001 Leuven, Belgium
- **Structure:** Mutual (both parties can be Discloser or Recipient)
- **Governing law:** Belgian law
- **Jurisdiction:** Courts of Antwerp
- **Term:** Until terminated; 30 days written notice to terminate
- **Survival:** Obligations survive until info becomes public OR 2 years from disclosure date (whichever earlier)

## Sections
1. Confidential Information (definition + exclusions)
2. Treatment (protection obligations, permitted use, disclosure under law)
3. Ownership (all CI remains Discloser's; no IP transfer)
4. Term
5. Termination (30-day notice)
6. Return of Confidential Information
7. Equitable Remedies
8. No Restrictions (non-exclusive, no obligation to transact)
9. Law, Jurisdiction, Venue and Attorney Fees
10. Severability
11. Assignment (written consent required)
12. Entire Agreement
13. Modifications (written only)
14. Waiver

## Placeholders to Fill
- `[name/number/registered address]`: counterparty legal details
- `___`: Effective Date
- Signature block for both SettleMint NV and the counterparty (name, title, address, date)

## Usage
When generating an NDA for a counterparty, use this template via the word-writer agent. Populate the counterparty details and effective date. Do not alter the SettleMint NV details or governing law/jurisdiction clauses without legal approval.
