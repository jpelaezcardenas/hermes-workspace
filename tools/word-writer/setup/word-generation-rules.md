# Word Generation Rules

1. Draft in markdown, deliver in DOCX.
2. Use the local conversion script, not ad hoc copy-paste into Word.
3. Default to `--mode simple --toc` for reports and other navigation-heavy documents.
4. Use `--no-toc` for short memos, meeting notes, and compact summaries.
5. Use `--mode full` only when a formal title-page document is appropriate.
6. Keep H1-H4 only.
7. Keep tables narrow enough to remain readable on A4.
8. Keep figures centered and captioned when they carry meaning.
9. Prevent blank pages, double page breaks, and dead whitespace before delivery.
10. Generate the DOCX, then review the DOCX, not just the markdown.
11. Do not let formatting features outrun the actual value of the content.
12. If the Word output feels heavier than the message, simplify it.
