# Title and Subtitle Standards

## Title
The title should be:
- specific
- plain-English
- outcome-oriented when possible
- short enough to scan fast in Word's page view

## Subtitle
Use the subtitle line for one of these only:
- date / period
- audience
- version / status
- scope qualifier

Do not cram multiple metadata fragments into a messy subtitle if a short intro paragraph can do the job better.

## Patterns
- `[Title]` + `[Date or period]`
- `[Title]` + `[Audience]`
- `[Title]` + `[Status | Date]`

## Good
- Digital Asset Operations Brief
- Executive Memo: Tokenization Readiness Review
- Q1 2026 Platform Delivery Summary
- Meeting Summary: Partner Enablement Review

## Bad
- Document
- Final Version Updated Latest
- Important Strategic Overview About Multiple Topics
- Notes
