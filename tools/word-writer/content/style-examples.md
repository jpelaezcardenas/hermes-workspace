# Style Examples

## Direct executive opening
**Weak**
This document provides an overview of several topics that may be relevant to leadership as part of the broader discussion.

**Better**
This memo recommends delaying external launch by two weeks so the onboarding flow can be fixed before broader exposure.

## Better section heading
**Weak**
## Overview

**Better**
## What changed since last review

## Better action item
**Weak**
- Follow up with team

**Better**
- Product to confirm revised onboarding copy by 19 March 2026.

## Better Q&A answer
**Weak**
It depends on several factors and there are various considerations to take into account.

**Better**
Yes, with one caveat: the workflow is ready for internal use, but external release should wait until the approval step is simplified.
