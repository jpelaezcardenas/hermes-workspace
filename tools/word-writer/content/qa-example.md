# Word Writer Q&A
15 March 2026

## Context
Common questions about the agent.

## Q1: When should I use Word Writer?
Use it for generic DOCX output such as reports, briefs, memos, meeting summaries, research summaries, and formatted internal or client-ready writeups.

## Q2: When should I not use it?
Do not use it for proposal responses, RFP generation, or press/media content.

## Q3: Should every document include a table of contents?
No. Use a TOC when the document is long or reference-heavy. Skip it for short decision memos and meeting notes.
