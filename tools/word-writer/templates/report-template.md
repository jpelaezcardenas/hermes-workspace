## Findings

<!-- PURPOSE: Present evidence and analysis. This is the core of the report.
     STRUCTURE: Use sub-headings (### Finding 1, ### Finding 2) when there are

     TABLES: Use tables for structured comparisons (features, vendors, criteria).
