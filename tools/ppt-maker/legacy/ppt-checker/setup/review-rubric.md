Use `setup/scoring-rubric.md` as the **canonical scoring rubric** for PPTX Reviewer.

- the 10 review dimensions
- full 1–5 calibration guidance for each dimension
- total score = **/50**
- verdict thresholds:

  - **30 to 39** → Revise
