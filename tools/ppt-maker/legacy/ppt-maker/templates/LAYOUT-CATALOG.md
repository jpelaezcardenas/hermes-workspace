# Layout Catalog — SettleMint Official Template

**Template:** `Master Template 2026.pptx`

**Slide Master:** 1 master, 36 layouts
**Font:** Figtree (Regular, Medium, SemiBold, ExtraBold, Light)

**Source:** Gyan-approved official SettleMint template (deployed 2026-03-15)

## Branded / Special Layouts (Slides 1–4)

These slides have branded background images and the SettleMint logo. **Do NOT modify** background images, logo placement, or footer/slide number shapes.

### Slide 1 — Title - Standard (Cover)
- **Purpose:** Opening/cover slide
- **Editable:** Title (presentation name), Subtitle (date, subtitle text)
- **Do NOT modify:** Background image (Cover.png), Logo, footer
- **Content guidance:** Title ≤8 words. Subtitle ≤15 words. Keep it punchy.

### Slide 2 — Section Divider - Bold
- **Purpose:** Major section break
- **Editable:** Title (section name), Body (section description, optional)
- **Do NOT modify:** Background image (Section.png), Logo, footer
- **Content guidance:** Title ≤5 words. Body ≤25 words or omit.

### Slide 3 — Section Divider - Numbered
- **Purpose:** Numbered section break (e.g., "01", "02")
- **Editable:** Number text (e.g., "01"), Title (section name)
- **Do NOT modify:** Background image (Section.png), Logo, footer
- **Content guidance:** Number is 2 digits. Title ≤5 words.

### Slide 4 — Closing - Thank You
- **Purpose:** CTA / closing / thank you slide
- **Editable:** Title (e.g., "Thank You"), Body (contact info, next steps)
- **Do NOT modify:** Background image (ThankYou.png), Logo, footer
- **Content guidance:** Title ≤4 words. Body ≤30 words with clear CTA.

## Content Layouts (Slides 5–36)

All content slides have white backgrounds, the "SettleMint Confidential" footer, and slide numbers. **Do NOT modify** footer or slide number shapes.

### Slide 5 — Agenda / TOC
- **Purpose:** Table of contents, meeting agenda
- **Editable:** Title, Body (full width)
- **Content guidance:** Title = "Agenda" or similar. Body: numbered list of 3–6 items. ≤8 words per item.

### Slide 6 — 1-Column Content
- **Purpose:** General single-column content
- **Editable:** Title, Body (full width)
- **Content guidance:** Title states the takeaway. Body: 3–6 bullets or 2–3 short paragraphs. Max ~80 words total.

### Slide 7 — 2-Column Equal
- **Purpose:** Side-by-side content, dual topics
- **Editable:** Title, Column 1 (left), Column 2 (right)
- **Content guidance:** Title states the comparison or connection. Each column: 3–5 bullets, ≤50 words. Columns should be parallel in structure.

### Slide 8 — 2-Column Wide-Narrow
- **Purpose:** Primary content with sidebar detail
- **Editable:** Title, Wide column (main content), Narrow column (supporting detail)
- **Content guidance:** Wide column carries the narrative (≤60 words). Narrow column provides stats, links, or callouts (≤30 words).

### Slide 9 — 3-Column Content
- **Purpose:** Three parallel topics, feature triplets
- **Editable:** Title, Col 1, Col 2, Col 3
- **Content guidance:** Title ≤8 words. Each column: heading + 2–4 bullets, ≤40 words per column.

### Slide 10 — Text Left + Image Right
- **Purpose:** Narrative with visual proof
- **Editable:** Title, Text area (left), Image area (right)
- **Content guidance:** Title states the takeaway. Text: 3–5 bullets or short paragraph (≤60 words). Image: screenshot, diagram, or photo.

### Slide 11 — Image Left + Text Right
- **Purpose:** Visual-led with explanation
- **Editable:** Title, Image area (left), Text area (right)
- **Content guidance:** Mirror of slide 10. Image draws the eye first, text explains.

### Slide 12 — Text + Image Top
- **Purpose:** Image header with text explanation below
- **Editable:** Title, Image area (top), Text (bottom)
- **Content guidance:** Title ≤8 words. Image: wide banner/screenshot. Text: ≤60 words.

### Slide 13 — Full Visual - Bleed
- **Purpose:** Hero image, full-bleed visual statement
- **Editable:** Title overlay, Full image
- **Content guidance:** Title ≤6 words (overlay on image). Image should be high-res, full-bleed.

### Slide 14 — Screenshot + Caption
- **Purpose:** Product screenshot with explanation
- **Editable:** Title, Screenshot area, Caption
- **Content guidance:** Title ≤8 words. Caption: 1–2 sentences explaining what the screenshot shows (≤30 words).

### Slide 15 — Stats - 2 Numbers
- **Purpose:** Two key metrics or KPIs
- **Editable:** Title, Stat 1 (number + label), Stat 2 (number + label)
- **Content guidance:** Title frames the story. Stats: large number + ≤8 word description each.

### Slide 16 — Stats - 3 Numbers
- **Purpose:** Three key metrics
- **Editable:** Title, Stat 1, Stat 2, Stat 3
- **Content guidance:** Same as slide 15 but with three stats. Keep labels tight (≤6 words each).

### Slide 17 — Stats - 4 Numbers
- **Purpose:** Four key metrics / dashboard summary
- **Editable:** Title, Stat 1, Stat 2, Stat 3, Stat 4
- **Content guidance:** Title ≤8 words. Each stat: number + ≤5 word label.

### Slide 18 — Chart Left + Text
- **Purpose:** Data visualization with narrative
- **Editable:** Title, Chart area (left), Text (right)
- **Content guidance:** Title states the insight. Chart: bar/line/pie. Text: 2–4 bullets explaining the data (≤50 words).

### Slide 19 — Chart Full Width
- **Purpose:** Full-width data visualization
- **Editable:** Title, Full chart area
- **Content guidance:** Title states the insight. Chart fills the slide. Minimal text — let the data speak.

### Slide 20 — Table - Simple
- **Purpose:** Structured data, feature matrix
- **Editable:** Title, Table
- **Content guidance:** Title ≤8 words. Table: max 6 rows × 5 columns. Keep cell text ≤15 words.

### Slide 21 — Table - Comparison
- **Purpose:** Feature comparison, vendor comparison
- **Editable:** Title, Table
- **Content guidance:** Same as slide 20 but designed for comparison layout with checkmarks/icons.

### Slide 22 — Process - Horizontal
- **Purpose:** Sequential process, workflow
- **Editable:** Title, Steps 1–4, Notes
- **Content guidance:** Title ≤8 words. Each step: short label (≤4 words) + description (≤15 words). Notes: ≤30 words.

### Slide 23 — Process - Vertical
- **Purpose:** Vertical process flow
- **Editable:** Title, Steps 1–4, Notes
- **Content guidance:** Same as slide 22 but vertical orientation.

### Slide 24 — Timeline - Horizontal
- **Purpose:** Quarterly or phase timeline, roadmap
- **Editable:** Title, Q1–Q4 markers (or phase labels), Notes
- **Content guidance:** Title ≤8 words. Each marker: phase label + 2–3 bullet items (≤20 words each). Notes: ≤30 words.

### Slide 25 — Architecture - Stack
- **Purpose:** Layered architecture diagram
- **Editable:** Title, 4 layer boxes (top to bottom), Notes
- **Content guidance:** Title ≤8 words. Each layer: label (≤4 words) + brief description (≤15 words). Notes: ≤30 words.

### Slide 26 — Architecture - Flow
- **Purpose:** Flow-based architecture diagram
- **Editable:** Title, 4 flow boxes (left to right), Notes
- **Content guidance:** Same as slide 25 but horizontal flow. Show data/process movement.

### Slide 27 — Comparison - Side by Side
- **Purpose:** Two options, before/after, us vs them
- **Editable:** Title, Left panel, Right panel
- **Content guidance:** Title frames the comparison. Each panel: heading + 3–5 bullets (≤50 words per panel).

### Slide 28 — Comparison - Pros/Cons
- **Purpose:** Advantages vs disadvantages, trade-offs
- **Editable:** Title, Pros panel, Cons panel
- **Content guidance:** Title ≤8 words. Each panel: 3–5 items (≤8 words each).

### Slide 29 — Quote / Testimonial
- **Purpose:** Customer quote, executive testimonial
- **Editable:** Title (optional), Quote text, Attribution (name, title, company)
- **Content guidance:** Quote: ≤40 words, compelling and specific. Attribution: full name + role + company.

### Slide 30 — Team / People Grid
- **Purpose:** Team introduction, leadership
- **Editable:** Title, 4 person cards (name, role, photo area each)
- **Content guidance:** Title ≤5 words. Each card: name, role (≤6 words), optional 1-line bio.

### Slide 31 — Icon Grid - 3 Column
- **Purpose:** Feature highlights with icons
- **Editable:** Title, 3 blocks (icon + heading + description each)
- **Content guidance:** Title ≤8 words. Each block: heading (≤4 words) + description (≤20 words).

### Slide 32 — Icon Grid - 4 Column
- **Purpose:** Four feature highlights with icons
- **Editable:** Title, 4 blocks (icon + heading + description each)
- **Content guidance:** Same as slide 31 but 4 columns. Keep descriptions tighter (≤15 words each).

### Slide 33 — Case Study
- **Purpose:** Customer success story
- **Editable:** Title, Challenge section, Solution section, Result section
- **Content guidance:** Title = customer name or case name. Each section: heading + 2–3 bullets (≤30 words per section).

### Slide 34 — Bullet List - Simple
- **Purpose:** Simple bullet point list
- **Editable:** Title, Bullets (full width)
- **Content guidance:** Title states the takeaway. 4–6 bullets, ≤10 words each. One idea per bullet.

### Slide 35 — Bullet List - Icon
- **Purpose:** Bullet list with icon markers
- **Editable:** Title, Bullet items with icon markers
- **Content guidance:** Same as slide 34 but each bullet has an icon/marker prefix. ≤8 words per item.

### Slide 36 — Data Story - Insight
- **Purpose:** Data-driven insight with supporting chart
- **Editable:** Title, Insight text (the "so what"), Chart area
- **Content guidance:** Title ≤8 words. Insight: 1–2 sentences stating the conclusion (≤25 words). Chart supports the insight.

## Shape Modification Rules

### Never Modify These Shapes (on any slide):
- Background images (Cover.png, Section.png, ThankYou.png on slides 1–4)
- SettleMint logo
- "SettleMint Confidential" footer text
- Slide number placeholder (`‹#›`)

### Always Modify These Shapes:
- Title text (`<a:t>` in title placeholder)
- Body/content text (`<a:t>` in body placeholders)
- Column text in multi-column layouts
- Stat numbers and labels
- Table content

### Modification by Replacement Only:
- Replace placeholder text in `<a:t>` tags
- Do NOT alter shape positions (`<a:off>`, `<a:ext>`)
- Do NOT alter font definitions (`<a:rPr>`, `<a:latin>`)
- Do NOT alter fill colors (`<a:solidFill>`)
- Do NOT alter group structure (`<p:grpSp>`)
