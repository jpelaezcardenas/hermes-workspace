# SettleMint Company Profile

## Company Overview

SettleMint is an enterprise blockchain technology company that builds infrastructure for regulated institutions to adopt digital asset management. The company's flagship product, the Digital Asset Lifecycle Platform (DALP), provides production-ready software for managing the complete lifecycle of tokenized securities.

SettleMint's fundamental belief is that doing tokenization the right way is difficult, and institutions should not have to become blockchain experts to benefit from it. DALP abstracts the complexity of blockchain infrastructure while giving institutions full control over compliance, custody, settlement, and operations.

## Mission

Enable regulated financial institutions to deploy tokenized asset infrastructure that meets institutional requirements for compliance, security, custody, and operational control, without requiring deep blockchain expertise or multi-vendor integration projects.

## Product Focus

SettleMint DALP addresses the full spectrum of digital asset management:

- **Asset issuance**: Production-ready templates for bonds, equities, funds, stablecoins, deposits, real estate, and precious metals.
- **Compliance**: ERC-3643 embedded compliance with jurisdictional rule support (US, EU, Singapore, UK, GCC).
- **Custody and settlement**: Multi-signature vault custody with atomic DvP/XvP settlement.
- **Servicing**: Automated yield management, corporate actions, and investor self-service.
- **Integration**: API-first design with typed REST endpoints, webhooks, SDKs, and pluggable adapters for KYC, banking, custody, and identity management systems.
- **Operations**: Integrated observability, enterprise deployment options, and white-label customization.

## Target Market

Regulated financial institutions including:

- Banks and financial services firms
- Asset managers and fund administrators
- Securities issuers and investment companies
- Central securities depositories
- Government agencies and sovereign wealth funds
- Fintech companies with institutional clients

## Geographic Presence

Active deployments and engagements across Europe (leveraging MiCA frameworks), Singapore (under MAS oversight), Gulf Cooperation Council markets, and expanding into other jurisdictions with clear tokenization regulatory guidance.

## Technology Approach

SettleMint's approach emphasizes:

- **Production readiness over experimentation**: Working software with operational infrastructure, not proof-of-concept tools.
- **Compliance-first architecture**: Regulatory controls embedded at the protocol level, not bolted on afterward.
- **API-first integration**: Every capability accessible through typed REST APIs, designed to fit into existing institutional technology landscapes.
- **Institutional adaptability**: Flexible deployment models (on-premises, cloud, SaaS) that conform to enterprise IT standards.
- **Open standards**: ERC-3643, ISO 20022, OpenID Connect, OAuth 2.0, enabling interoperability with existing financial infrastructure.

## Engagement Model

SettleMint offers platform licensing, implementation services, and ongoing support. Typical engagements progress from initial assessment and proof of concept (4 to 8 weeks for the first asset type) to production deployment with enterprise integration, followed by ongoing support and expansion to additional asset classes (2 to 4 weeks per additional type).
