# DALP RFI Question Bank

This question bank is a reusable response library for RFIs, due diligence questionnaires, and security questionnaires related to DALP, the Digital Asset Lifecycle Platform. Answers are written to be bid-safe, technically defensible, and easy to tailor per opportunity.

Use the confidence flags strictly:

- **✅ Verified**: grounded in current DALP product documentation, architecture material, capability mapping, or confirmed workaround analysis
- **🟡 Partial**: partly supported, but the final answer should be tailored to the target operating model, partner stack, or deployment scope
- **❌ Gap**: not a native DALP capability today, or not something that should be claimed as product functionality

> **Important:** DALP is a platform for digital asset lifecycle management. It is not a bank, not a custodian, not a payment rail, and not a regulatory intelligence service. Where a requirement belongs to infrastructure, custody, or external compliance providers, the answer should say so plainly.

## Tailoring Notes for Bid Managers

- Use **✅ Verified** answers as the default baseline.
- Use **🟡 Partial** answers when the evaluator's requirement depends on the final deployment, custody model, infrastructure scope, or external provider stack.
- Use **❌ Gap** answers without trying to hide the limitation. In regulated bids, honesty buys more credibility than hand-waving.
- Where the question is really about infrastructure, custody, or external compliance intelligence, say that plainly.
- Where the question asks for company facts such as insurance, audited financials, certifications, or named references, complete the answer from the approved corporate response pack rather than improvising.

DALP is strongest when positioned as an institutional digital asset lifecycle platform with:
