## Pricing Rules

**Payment terms:** Annual, upfront. No monthly payment option.

**Taxes:** All prices exclude applicable taxes and VAT. Taxes are added separately based on the client's jurisdiction and applicable tax treaties. This exclusion must be stated clearly in every commercial proposal.

**Discounts:** Not offered without explicit Gyan/Roderik approval. Do not suggest, imply, or hint at discounts in any proposal or conversation. Do not use language like "competitive pricing available" or "volume discounts may apply", these create negotiation expectations.

**Multi-environment pricing:**
- Production + Development combined = €420,000/year upfront
- Additional environments beyond the base tier are priced per the license type (production or development)

**Infrastructure costs:**
- Cloud-managed: pass-through at cost, not marked up by SettleMint
- On-premises: borne entirely by the client
- Infrastructure costs are always separate from the platform license

**Implementation services:** Priced separately per engagement. Always marked [CLIENT-SPECIFIC] in proposals until scoped.

**Support & maintenance:** Included or add-on depending on tier selection. Always stated clearly alongside the license.

## Usage in Proposals

When writing commercial proposals or sections containing pricing:

1. **Always use the exact figures above**: €25,000/month production, €10,000/month development
2. **Always state "All prices exclude applicable taxes and VAT"** in the pricing section
3. **Always state "Annual, upfront"** as the payment structure
4. **Never round down**: €25,000 is €25,000, not "approximately €25K" or "starting from €20K"
5. **Never suggest flexibility** on the base pricing without Gyan/Roderik approval
6. **Mark implementation and support as [CLIENT-SPECIFIC]**: these are scoped per deal
7. **Reference this file** in the commercial proposal's metadata or build notes

## Tier Pricing Guidelines

The base licensing prices above apply across all tiers. What varies by tier is:
- Number of environments included
- Support level included
- Implementation scope and therefore implementation pricing
- Custom SLA terms (Sovereign/Strategic only)

The **platform license rate** (€25K production, €10K development per month) does not change between Foundation, Enterprise, and Sovereign tiers. The total deal value changes based on how many environments, what support tier, and what implementation scope is included.
