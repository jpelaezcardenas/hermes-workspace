# Request for Information

## Tokenized Gold Platform

**Issued by:** AurelionVault Commodities Trading DMCC and SwissSecure Bullion AG

**RFI Reference:** AV-RFI-2026-003

**Date of Issue:** 18 March 2026

**Response Deadline:** 15 April 2026

---

## Background

AurelionVault Commodities Trading DMCC ("AurelionVault"), a Dubai-based commodities trading house licensed by the Dubai Multi Commodities Centre (DMCC), and SwissSecure Bullion AG ("SwissSecure"), an LBMA-accredited Good Delivery gold refiner headquartered in Zurich, intend to jointly launch a gold-backed digital token platform. The platform will enable institutional and qualified retail investors to acquire, hold, and trade digital tokens backed by physical gold held in secure vaults operated by SwissSecure in Zurich and AurelionVault in Dubai.

The joint venture seeks a technology platform capable of supporting both allocated gold (where specific bars are assigned to individual token holders) and unallocated gold (where token holders have a pooled claim on vault inventory), with transparent collateral verification and secondary market transferability.

The target investor base includes institutional participants (sovereign wealth funds, family offices, asset managers) and qualified retail investors in the UAE, Switzerland, and the broader GCC region. The platform must comply with DMCC regulations, FINMA requirements for precious metals-linked financial products, and LBMA Good Delivery standards.

## Scope of Information Requested

Respondents are requested to address the following areas in detail.

### Section 1: Platform Capabilities

1.1. Describe how your platform supports the issuance of gold-backed digital tokens, including the relationship between physical gold inventory and token supply.

1.2. Explain how your platform differentiates between allocated gold (specific bars assigned to holders) and unallocated gold (pooled claims), and whether holders can convert between these two structures.

1.3. Describe the collateral verification mechanisms your platform provides. How does the platform ensure that token supply never exceeds verified physical gold holdings?

1.4. Explain how your platform integrates with external precious metals pricing sources (LBMA Gold Price, Comex, Reuters). Describe how pricing data staleness is detected and how the platform responds to price feed failures or divergences.

1.5. Describe your platform's support for physical redemption workflows. When a token holder wishes to take physical delivery of gold, what steps does the platform manage, and what steps fall outside the platform's scope?

### Section 2: Compliance and Regulatory

2.1. Describe how your platform enforces compliance across two jurisdictions simultaneously: DMCC regulations for Dubai-vaulted gold and FINMA requirements for Zurich-vaulted gold.

2.2. Explain how investor eligibility is verified and enforced. How does the platform handle tiered investor categories (institutional with enhanced due diligence versus qualified retail with simplified KYC)?

2.3. Describe AML/CFT capabilities, including ongoing transaction monitoring, sanctions screening integration, and suspicious transaction reporting.

2.4. How does the platform support audit requirements? Describe the proof-of-reserve attestation workflow and how audit results are made available to investors and regulators.

### Section 3: Operations and Integration

3.1. Describe how your platform integrates with existing vault management systems for inventory reconciliation. What level of automation is achievable versus what requires manual operational processes?

3.2. Explain secondary market transfer capabilities. Can token holders trade their positions peer-to-peer, and what compliance checks are enforced during transfers?

3.3. Describe the platform's approach to NAV calculation and reporting for gold-backed tokens.

3.4. How does the platform support multi-currency settlement for token purchases and redemptions?

### Section 4: Technical Architecture

4.1. Describe the blockchain infrastructure underlying your platform, including the consensus mechanism, permissioning model, and network topology.

4.2. What are the platform's availability and disaster recovery specifications?

4.3. Describe API capabilities for integration with the joint venture's existing trading and risk management systems.

4.4. How does the platform handle data residency requirements across the two operating jurisdictions (UAE and Switzerland)?

### Section 5: Implementation

5.1. Provide a high-level implementation timeline for the described platform, from contract signing to first gold token issuance.

5.2. Describe the ongoing support and maintenance model.

5.3. What is your pricing model (licensing, transaction-based, or hybrid)?
