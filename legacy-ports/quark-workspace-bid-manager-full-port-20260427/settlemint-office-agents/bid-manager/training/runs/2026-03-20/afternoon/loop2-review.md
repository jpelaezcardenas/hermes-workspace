## Top 3 Weaknesses

1. **Missing explicit win theme callouts** — The themes are present implicitly but not as forcefully woven as they could be. A dedicated "Why DALP for Brickstone" section or explicit differentiator paragraph near the front would strengthen evaluator recall.

3. **No competitive differentiation framing** — The response describes DALP's capabilities well but does not position them against the alternative approaches Brickstone might be considering (custom-built stack, point-solution assembly, consulting engagement). A brief competitive context paragraph would sharpen the value proposition.
