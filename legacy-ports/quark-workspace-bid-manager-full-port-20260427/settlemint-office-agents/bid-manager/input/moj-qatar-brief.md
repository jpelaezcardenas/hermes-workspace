# MOJ Qatar: TITAN Proposal Brief

## Opportunity
- **Client**: Ministry of Justice (MoJ), State of Qatar
- **Project**: Blockchain-based real estate tokenization and compliance platform
- **Partner**: Malomatia (system integrator; SettleMint is the technology/platform partner)
- **Key system integration**: SAK (national land registry)
- **Submission deadline**: March 19, 2026
- **Contact**: Gyan Sharma, VP Digital Assets MEA, gyan@settlemint.com, +971-564500007, DIFC Dubai

## Scope
- Real estate tokenization using DALP platform
- ERC-3643 compliant tokens with embedded compliance
- Integration with SAK land registry, KYC/AML providers, banks, custodians
- Deployment on permissioned EVM chain (Hyperledger Besu)
- Full lifecycle: issuance, compliance, custody, settlement, servicing, reporting
- Arabic/English localization
- Aligned with Qatar Financial Centre rules, investment token regulations, token service provider guidelines

## Commercial Context (from commercial proposal)
- 5-year contract: 2 years implementation + 3 years production
- Dev license: €2,500/month (Year 2-5)
- Production license: €26,000/month (Year 3-5)
- Professional services: 60 mandates at €700/day = €42,000
- Total 5-year: €1,098,000 / QAR 4,831,200
- No per-wallet or per-transaction fees
- HA/DR included in production license
- Infrastructure costs borne by Ministry
