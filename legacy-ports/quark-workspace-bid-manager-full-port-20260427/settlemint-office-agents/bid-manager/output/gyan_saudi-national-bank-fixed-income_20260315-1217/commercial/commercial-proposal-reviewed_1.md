## Executive Summary

Saudi National Bank requires a platform and delivery partner to bring tokenized fixed income origination and investor servicing to production. This commercial proposal presents SettleMint's licensing, implementation, and support model for DALP, structured for predictable costs, transparent assumptions, and clear TCO across a three-year horizon.

**Recommendation:** Enterprise license (Tier 2), dedicated cloud KSA-resident, 19-week implementation, Premium support (16x5, Sunday-Thursday alignment).

The DALP licensing model charges a predictable annual subscription with no per-transaction, per-token, per-user, or per-instrument fees. SNB can scale issuance volume, instrument types, and investor base without incremental platform costs. Coupon distributions and compliance checks carry zero marginal licensing cost.
