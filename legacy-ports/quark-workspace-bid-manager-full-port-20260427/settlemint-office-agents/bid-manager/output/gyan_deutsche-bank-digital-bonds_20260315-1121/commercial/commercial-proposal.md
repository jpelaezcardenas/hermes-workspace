## Executive Summary

Deutsche Bank requires a production-grade digital bonds issuance platform that operates within the eWpG and MiCA regulatory framework. This commercial proposal presents SettleMint's licensing, implementation, and support model for DALP.

**Recommendation:** Enterprise tier (Tier 2) with Premium support, 19-week fixed-fee implementation, EU-hosted deployment.
