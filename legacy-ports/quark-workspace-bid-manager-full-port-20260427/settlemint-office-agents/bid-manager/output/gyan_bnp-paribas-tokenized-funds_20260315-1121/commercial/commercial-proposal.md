## Executive Summary

BNP Paribas requires a tokenized fund distribution platform to modernize its asset servicing operations. This commercial proposal presents DALP's licensing, implementation, and support model.

**Recommendation:** Enterprise tier (Tier 2) with Premium support, 19-week fixed-fee implementation, EU-hosted deployment.
