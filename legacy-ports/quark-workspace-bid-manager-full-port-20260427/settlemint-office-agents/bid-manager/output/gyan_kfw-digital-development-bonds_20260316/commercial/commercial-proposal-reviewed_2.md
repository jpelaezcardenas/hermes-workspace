## Executive Summary

SettleMint is pleased to submit this commercial proposal in response to KfW's Request for Proposal for a Digital Development Bonds Platform. This proposal presents a transparent, value-based commercial model designed to support KfW's production operating requirements while maintaining cost predictability as the platform scales.

Our commercial approach is grounded in the following principles:

- **Transparency**: Clear separation of costs with explicit assumptions
- **Predictability**: Recurring costs that scale predictably with usage
- **Value**: Pricing aligned to the operational value delivered, not arbitrary metrics
- **Flexibility**: Commercial models that adapt to KfW's specific operating model

This proposal assumes a managed cloud deployment model with Premium support, designed to meet KfW's requirements for production operating capability, regulatory compliance, and governance alignment.

---

# 1. Commercial Overview

## 1.1 Pricing Philosophy

SettleMint's commercial model reflects our platform approach rather than a custom development engagement. We price for:

1. **Platform access**: The software capability to manage digital development bonds
2. **Implementation services**: The one-time effort to configure and deploy the platform
3. **Operational support**: The ongoing services to maintain production operations

We do not charge based on transaction volumes or assets under management, as we believe this aligns incentives appropriately: KfW benefits from operational efficiency without punitive scaling costs.

## 1.2 Cost Categories

| Category | Description | Model |
|---|---|---|
| **Software Licensing** | DALP platform access | Monthly subscription |
| **Implementation Services** | Configuration, integration, testing | One-time project fee |
| **Environment Costs** | Cloud infrastructure | Monthly (pass-through) |
| **Support Services** | Operational support | Monthly subscription |
| **Optional Services** | Training, additional modules | As quoted |
