## Executive Summary

UBS is establishing a platform for tokenized equities trading and settlement to modernize its equity markets infrastructure and serve institutional investors through digital channels.

SettleMint's DALP provides complete equity lifecycle management with exchange connectivity and settlement integration.

**Key Points:**

- Equity tokenization
- Exchange integration
- T+0 settlement
- Regulatory compliance (FINMA, SEC)
