## 8. Build vs Buy

Custom build estimate: AUD 8-15 million plus AUD 1.5-3 million/year maintenance. DALP 5-year cost: approximately AUD 4.5-5.2 million (at 1.48 AUD/EUR). Savings over 5 years: AUD 8-18 million.

---

## 9. ROI Analysis

Key quantified savings annually at AUD 10B RMBS programme scale: servicer report assembly automation (AUD 500,000/year staff cost saved); T+0 settlement counterparty risk reduction (AUD 600,000/year expected loss reduction); APRA examination preparation (AUD 120,000/year saved); reconciliation elimination (AUD 350,000/year). Total annual savings at scale: approximately AUD 1.6 million/year. Payback period: under 18 months from go-live.
